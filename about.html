hi there!


my name is Rachel but you can call me Rae


I started practicing yoga in Vancouver, Canada when I was about 15 years old, but my practice was not consistent. As the years went by my practice would slowly gain consistency but it wasn't until around 2018 in Perth, Australia that I began my daily practice. I began to slowly notice changes sneak into my daily life, the way I viewed myself and the world. I slowly became a little more patient, more gentle and more introspective. I started my teacher training just before the pandemic took hold in 2020, we paused and started again 6 months later.
