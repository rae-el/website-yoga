classes

classes offered


All classes are designed to bring you into the moment and focus on the breath. I aim to build flexibility in the body and the mind and have a little fun along the way.

Each class style has a different focus and method. Have a look below to see which style may suit what you're looking for. If you're unsure send me an enquiry and I'll try and point you in the right direction




Style Method Focus
Vinyasa Flow through movements with breath Strength & balance
Slow Flow Slowly flow through movements with time for pause and reflection Strength & stretch
VinYin Slowly flow through movements followed by long posture holds Strength, stretch & relaxation
Yin Long posture holds followed by meditation Stretch & relax
